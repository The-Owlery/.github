# The Owlery

Welcome to The Owlery — a shared private space for the community behind the **Rest of the Owl** Discord server.

## What is this?

The Owlery is a GitHub organization for a close-knit group of friends and fellow developers. It exists so members can share code, collaborate on projects, and learn together without cluttering their personal profiles with works-in-progress, experiments, or community projects.

Everything here is private and shared only among members of the group.

## Who is this for?

This organization is home to members of the **Rest of the Owl** Discord — a community dedicated to helping junior developers grow into senior engineers. If you're here, you're part of a group that believes in learning in the open (at least among friends), sharing knowledge generously, and lifting each other up.

## What belongs here?

- Side projects you want feedback on
- Experiments and learning exercises
- Collaborative tools built for or by the community
- Code you want to share with the group without publishing publicly

## The spirit of the space

The Owlery is built on the same values as the Discord: **curiosity, generosity, and growth**. Share your messy code. Ask dumb questions. Review each other's PRs. The goal isn't perfection — it's progress.

Junior or senior, everyone here is still learning. Act like it.
