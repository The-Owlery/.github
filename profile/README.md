# The Owlery

Welcome to The Owlery — a shared private space for the community behind the **Rest of the Owl** Discord server.

## What is this?

The Owlery is a GitHub organization for a close-knit group of friends and fellow developers. It's a place to share code with each other privately — think snippets, examples, and fragments you want to show the group without publishing them to your personal profile.

This isn't a home for full projects. Keep those on your own profile. What belongs here is the stuff you want to share, get eyes on, or reference together as a group.

## Who is this for?

This organization is home to members of the **Rest of the Owl** Discord — a community dedicated to helping junior developers grow into senior engineers. If you're here, you're part of a group that believes in sharing knowledge generously and lifting each other up.

## What belongs here?

- Code snippets you want feedback on
- Examples you want to share with the group
- Fragments from something you're working on that sparked a conversation
- Occasional group collaborations, if something like that comes together

## What doesn't belong here?

Your full private projects. Those live on your own profile. The Owlery is for sharing, not building.

## The spirit of the space

The Owlery is built on the same values as the Discord: **curiosity, generosity, and growth**. Share your messy code. Ask dumb questions. Review each other's work. The goal isn't perfection — it's progress.

Junior or senior, everyone here is still learning. Act like it.
